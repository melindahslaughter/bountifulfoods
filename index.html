San Diego

San Diego is located on the Pacific coast of
California. It is known for its beaches, parks
and some of the best weather year round in
the United States. Immense Balboa Park is the
famous San Dieg Zoo, as well as many art
galleries, museums, art studios and gardens.
It is boasts Petco field, home of the San
Diego Padres. San Diego is home to a large
Naval fleet, with the museum the USS Midway,
a once active air craft carrier that is now
a museum open to the public.

San Clemente

San Clemente is locate in Orange County,
California. San Onofre and San Clemente
state beaches are located in San Clemente.
They are known for their surf breaks and
sandstone bluffs. The Beach Trail is perfect
for running along the coast with sea views
and green parks. San Clemente Pier located
near T-Street Beach, reaches into the Pacific
Ocean. The city's Spanish-style architecture
are showcased at the Casa Romantica Cultural
Center and Gardens.

Encinitas

Encinitas is a beach city located in the
North County area of San Diego County,
in Southern California. It is nintey-five
miles south of Los Angeles. It if famous
for some of the most beautiful beaches in
California. It has excellent surfing conditions
and every year it hosts surfing tournaments.
It has an eclectic downtown with many surf
shops, coffeehouses and record stores. It
has always been a sure draw for the unique
surf and hippie crowds.
